About me

My name is Jennie. I love animals, nature and the great outdoors.

I am an experienced pet and house sitter living in central Brighton and as I don't have my own pets I would love to give yours my undivided attention!

I have plenty of experience working with animals and I’m interested anything from bees to billy-goats. As well as pet-sitting I have also worked on city and rural farms and am a regular at my local community allotment project, where I spend a disproportionate amount of time staring into the murky waters of the wildlife ponds hoping to spot a newtlet or two.

While full of love and hugs for your pet, I understand that being away from their owners can be a disruptive time so take seriously their adherence to any routines or special requirements. I’m happy to be flexible and work according to their needs – from popping in to check on them to longer stays or taking your beloved pet for regular exercise.

I don’t take lots of pets on at once, so can guarantee you a personal service.

While you're away I can also water the plants and garden, get some basic shopping in or even bake you a cake to welcome you home.
